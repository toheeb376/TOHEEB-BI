What We Do

Six Ways We
Elevate Your Data

01
Business Intelligence Dashboards
Custom-built, interactive Streamlit and Power BI dashboards with full brand theming, KPI cards, Plotly visualizations (including 3D scatter plots), and executive insight panels — tailored to your industry and data.
02
Data Analytics & Reporting
End-to-end data pipeline development using Python and Pandas, transforming raw datasets into automated, scheduled reports with actionable insights for decision-makers.
03
Dashboard Deployment & DevOps
Full deployment readiness including GitHub repository structuring, requirements.txt documentation, Streamlit Cloud configuration, and production-grade error handling.
04
Prompt Engineering & AI Integration
Strategic AI prompt design for BI workflows, enabling faster prototyping, intelligent data summaries, and AI-augmented analytics within existing business systems.
05
Brand-Specific BI Theming
Pixel-perfect color theming, logo integration, and CSS customization to ensure every dashboard reflects your corporate identity with consistency and professionalism.
06
BI Consulting & Strategy
Advisory services for organizations building their analytics stack — from tool selection and architecture planning to team capacity building and data governance frameworks.
